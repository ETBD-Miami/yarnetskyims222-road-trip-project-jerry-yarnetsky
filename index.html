Shuffle Off to Buffalo

Everyone totally knows about Buffalo for its chicken wings, but there's so much more! First there are local cult classics such as Loganberry Drink, Sponge Candy, and Beef-on-Weck. It has a rich tapestry of immigrant food, both from early rounds of immigration during Buffalo's industrial boom years and again during the 21st century immigration from war-torn countries such as South Sudan.
